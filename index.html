How to get Appstate?


  1. First download WEBVIUM.
  2. Open the Webvium app and search for Facebook in the search bar, then log in to the account you want to use as a bot (it's better to use a dummy account for this).
  3. Once you log in to the account you want to use as a bot, click the three dots at the top side, then find "DevTools," and click on "Cookie Manager," then copy your "Appstate."
  4. After copying your Appstate, go back to the link, then paste your Appstate into the Appstate container, and also enter the Facebook ID for admin (this is optional) and a prefix (also optional).
  5. Then you have your bot. ☺️

IMPORTANT NOTE:

There is a possibility that the account you use as a bot may get locked or suspended, and I don't have responsibility for that, but not all accounts used as bots get locked or suspended. It's risky, but it's helpful for our academics.
